Pokemon Generator


Single Pokemon information

This is a site where you will be able to search up specific pokemon. This will give you the opppurtunity to learn more about the pokemon. Getting the specifics will help you in the pokemon video game. For example: Pokemon Red, Pokemon Moon, and Pokemon Ultra sun but, not Let's Go Eevee or Pikachu.


Team information

You will also be able to generate a team of pokemon. Going through each ones specifics and deciding what would be good for your team. There will also be a random team generator for a more random experience in the game. The pokemon will be shown based on what game you choose from. For example: Pokemon Red will only show the pokemon it has in that specific game.
