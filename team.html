Generate your Team!

This is the page where you are free to generate your team of pokemon. Just choose a game, then generate your team based off of pokemon that exist inside that game!


There are multiple ways that you can generate your pokemon. Allow them all to be generated at random, choose your own, or do a mix of the two. In the end, we are sure that you will have a team that you will enjoy having in your game.
